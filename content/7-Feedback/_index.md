### GENERAL EVALUATION

#### 1. Working Environment

As a new intern entering a professional workplace, I initially felt quite shy and unfamiliar with the new environment. I was especially impressed by how the colleagues were always willing to support me and patiently answer my questions whenever I encountered difficulties. The company's workspace is well-organized, which helped me maintain better focus and productivity throughout my internship.

#### 2. Support from Mentor / Admin Team

I feel very fortunate to have had the opportunity to work with a mentor who has an effective and thoughtful approach to guidance. This mentoring style helped me develop independence, critical thinking skills, and better problem-solving abilities.

In addition, the admin team provided enthusiastic support throughout my internship, including assistance with procedures, providing necessary materials, and creating favorable conditions for me to work with confidence.

#### 3. Relevance Between Work and Academic Background

During my internship, I not only had the opportunity to reinforce my fundamental knowledge related to my major but also gained exposure to new technologies and working processes that I had not previously learned at university.

Directly participating in real-world projects helped me gain a deeper understanding of theoretical concepts and identify areas where I still needed improvement so that I could continue developing myself.

#### 4. Learning Opportunities & Skill Development

I learned how to use project management tools more effectively, improve my teamwork skills, and develop professional communication skills in a corporate environment.

The internship experience also helped me improve my ability to collaborate, manage tasks, and adapt to practical working situations.

#### 5. Company Culture & Team Spirit

The culture at FCAJ has brought me a lot of positive energy. Everyone respects each other, works professionally, and maintains a friendly atmosphere without unnecessary pressure.

Especially during periods when projects entered critical stages, the team spirit was clearly demonstrated. All members worked together, supported one another, and collaborated regardless of position or level.

#### 6. Policies / Benefits for Interns

The company provides flexible working conditions, allowing interns to manage their time effectively while ensuring that work requirements are completed.

### OTHER QUESTIONS

* **What did you appreciate most during your internship?**

> The most valuable thing I gained was **confidence and independence**. During my internship, I overcame my fear of making mistakes, became more proactive in experimenting with solutions, and was able to independently solve practical problems related to projects.

* **Would you recommend this internship program to your friends? Why?**

> **Definitely yes.** FCAJ is an ideal environment for those who want to seriously begin their professional journey. Everyone is willing to listen, provide feedback, and help you improve. Most importantly, interns are given real opportunities to contribute, gain practical experience, and learn from real-world projects.
